My Story

In my sophmore year at Cal Poly Pomona, during the financial crisis of 2007-2008, I paused my university attendance to explore the workforce. Despite the increasing unemployment rate, I landed a full-time job in logistics. I worked at various companies over the next 7 years within the supply chain and got a good look at international trade and commerce.

I needed to combine my work experience with knowledge so in 2017, shortly after returning back to school, I received an associate degree in business from Chaffey College. My plan was to pursue accounting and finance but my intuition told me to pursue technology so I started majoring in Computer Science at California State University, San Bernardino.

I will graduate next summer and currently experimenting with web and mobile app technologies to showcase and express who I am, what I know, and how I can help. I aspire to become a great software engineer and very excited for the future.
