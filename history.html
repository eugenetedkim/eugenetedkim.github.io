Pomodoro Technique

I started implementing the Pomodoro Technique which is a time management method.
  1. Decide on the task to be done.
  2. Set the timer to 25 minutes.
  3. Work on the task.
  4. End work when the timer rings and put a checkmark on a piece of paper.
  5. If you have fewer than four checkmarks, take a short break (3-5 minutes), then go to step 2.
  6. After four pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes), resent your checkmark count to zero, then go to step 1.
